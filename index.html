Data Deletion Instructions for LapTech

At LapTech, we respect your privacy and offer the option to delete your data from our servers. Follow these steps to request the deletion of your data:

Step 1: Access Your Account

Log in to your LapTech account using your username and password.

Step 2: Navigate to Settings

Once logged in, go to the Settings or Account section. This is typically represented by a gear icon or labeled as "Settings."

Step 3: Locate Data Deletion Option

Look for the option labeled "Delete Account" or "Delete Data." Click on this option to proceed with the deletion process.

Step 4: Confirmation

You may be asked to confirm the deletion request. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion of your account or data.

Step 5: Confirmation Email

After initiating the deletion process, you will receive an email confirming your request. This email may include additional information or instructions.

Additional Information:

Data Retention:

Please note that while we promptly delete your account and associated data upon request, some residual data may remain in our backups or logs for a limited period due to technical constraints.

Contact Support:

If you encounter any issues or have specific queries regarding data deletion, feel free to contact our support team at help@laptech.com
